pick a strip length (2/3/4), choose photo or video mode, then hit "start strip" — the booth will count down and fill each slot automatically. video shots loop live right in the frame. saving exports photos as one flattened strip PNG, and video clips as one combined strip-shaped .webm video. on desktop, open .webm files in Chrome/Firefox/VLC rather than the default Windows/Mac player for best results.
